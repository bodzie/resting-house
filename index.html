The Resting House

Welcome to The Resting House, a high-end assisted-living facility with community spaces to engage the residents, their families and the communities around them.
We provide 24-hour permanent and respite care (post-hospitalisation) managed by nurses and caregivers in fully-furnished individual suites.
The Resting House offers the highest standard of care in a safe and comfortable environment.
Managed by Care Concierge (SEA Startup Disruptor 2019), family members from round the world have peace-of-mind with a tech app that provides daily tracking and updates of the residents.
If you would like to have the option of homecare services instead, where we send nurses, therapists and caregivers to your own home, please visit our partner’s page  to find out more.
