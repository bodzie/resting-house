About The Resting House

The Resting House represents a vast home. With our high-end assisted-living facilities, we aim to recreate the feeling of a sprawling mansion. Our community spaces are designed to engage the residents, their families and the communities around them. We currently have 4 premium homes across Petaling Jaya; 3 in Jalan Gasing (with a dedicated Dementia home) and our latest 28-bedder in Section 14. Our homes are all designed by an award-winning retirement village architect and the lawns are carefully designed and manicured to create the best living environment.
We provide 24-hour permanent and respite care (post-hospitalisation) managed by nurses and caregivers in fully-furnished individual suites, offering the highest standard of care in a safe and comfortable environment.
Managed by Care Concierge (SEA Startup Disruptor 2019), family members from around the world can be connected to their family members via a tech app that provides updates of the residents and their condition.
