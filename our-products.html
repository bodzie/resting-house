136 Gasing

136 Gasing is a 47-bedder assisted living facility across 3 adjoined homes located in Jalan Gasing. Built around matured landscaped grounds and surrounded by greenery, we have outdoor activity spaces, al fresco dining areas and we are located close to two hospitals.
We also have a dedicated dementia home for those looking for specialized care for their loved ones.

60 Gasing

The Resting House@60Gasing is our latest and largest assisted-living facility. We have 60 individual rooms equipped with our smart home system and have well-appointed outdoor areas for residents and visiting family members. With design inspirations from Bali for a mini retirement village feel, we have two courtyards which surrounds our facilities with calming elements of water and greenery, our signature across all facilities.

 

Located close to hospitals and managed by a high ratio of residents to caregivers, we are here to offer comfort and respite for all, whether it is long-term care or post-hospitalisation recovery. It is after all, home.

Section 14
